About

We are here to be a solution to grow your business in the digital world.

  • 2012

    The birth of Barock IT company

    Barock IT was founded in February 2012 in Bandung, Indonesia. Our mission is to provide all that clients deserve. We are a professional Developer Team who are very experienced in creating websites, web applications and mobile applications that your company needs. we always innovate continuously and always keep abreast of technological developments to provide the best results.

  • Tech Expert

    The Barock IT team consists of a group of people who share the same mind and interest in learning new technologies in the world. we always update continuously so that this technology can be applied in every project that is made.

  • Perfect Design

    Like our motto, we will make your dreams come true through an application design that is easy to use and of course has gone through the best research to get perfect results.

  • Executed quickly and precisely.

    After the design stage, we will carry out the execution in a fast and precise manner. we develop applications that are easy for users so it won't be confusing if you try it.
